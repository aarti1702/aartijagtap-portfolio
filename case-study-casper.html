Casper Case Study

As part of my marketing coursework, I analyzed Casper, a DTC company transforming the mattress industry with its "One Perfect Mattress for Everyone" model. The case study involved applying marketing frameworks to evaluate Casper’s strategies and propose actionable solutions.

Key highlights include:

  • Frameworks Applied: Used STP (Segmentation, Targeting, Positioning) to define and address three customer personas, and conducted a SWOT analysis to identify Casper’s strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats.
  • Market Insights: Assessed Casper’s competitive positioning in the $14 billion U.S. mattress industry and its innovative bed-in-a-box approach.
  • Strategic Recommendations: Proposed reallocating ad spend using ROI analysis and suggested expanding into new metro areas while maintaining a consistent customer experience.

This project honed my ability to apply marketing frameworks to real-world scenarios, enhancing my analytical and strategic skills.
