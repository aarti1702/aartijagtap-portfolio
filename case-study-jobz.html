A Design Thinking Journey with Jobz

As part of our Design Thinking and Innovation course, we partnered with Jobz, a career services platform designed to streamline the job search process for job seekers and employers. Throughout the semester, we applied the design thinking methodology to understand user pain points and identify opportunities for innovation.

Our process included:

  • User Research: Conducted surveys and interviews with job seekers and employers to identify pain points and understand user needs.
  • Persona Development: Created detailed user personas and empathy maps to guide design decisions.
  • Journey Mapping: Mapped the user journey to pinpoint critical touchpoints and areas for improvement.
  • Ideation: Generated innovative ideas to address user challenges and improve platform functionality.
  • Prototyping: Developed low-fidelity and high-fidelity prototypes, testing them with users for feedback and refinement.

In the final presentation, we shared actionable recommendations and design solutions aimed at improving user engagement, simplifying the job search process, and aligning Jobz’s platform with both user needs and business goals. This project not only gave us valuable hands-on experience but also allowed us to directly contribute to Jobz’s growth through innovation and design thinking.
